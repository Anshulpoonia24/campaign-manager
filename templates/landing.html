Frequently asked questions

Do you provide SMTP?+

No. OutreachOS connects to YOUR SMTP accounts (Gmail, Outlook, SES, Mailgun, SendGrid, or any SMTP provider). We orchestrate sending across multiple accounts with rotation and warmup.

Can I use Gmail / Google Workspace?+

Yes! Connect via SMTP (App Password) or any Google Workspace account. We recommend multiple accounts with rotation for best deliverability.

How does AI personalization work?+

Our AI researches each contact's company, role, and recent activity to generate unique email copy. Each email is different — no templates, no spam filters triggered.

Is there a free trial?+

Yes! Sign up for free and get 100 emails + full AI copilot access for 7 days. No credit card required.

Can agencies manage multiple clients?+

Absolutely. Each client gets an isolated workspace with their own SMTP, contacts, campaigns, and analytics. Switch between workspaces instantly.
